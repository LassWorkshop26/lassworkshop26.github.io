Social simulation matters where human studies face constraints — sociology, psychology, medicine, and education. Rule-based models often oversimplify behavior; LLM agents can interpret context, reason, and adapt in simulated societies. LASS 2026 focuses on social simulation with LLM agents: faithfully modeled, rigorously evaluated, safely governed, and applied in vertical domains.
